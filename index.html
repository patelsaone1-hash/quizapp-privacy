Privacy Policy

Quiz App · Effective date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Quiz App ("the app", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. This app is published by HimU ("the developer"). We respect your privacy and have designed this app to collect as little information as possible.

Short version: Quiz App is an offline trivia app. It does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information. It contains no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.

1. Information We Collect

Quiz App does not collect any personal information. Specifically, the app does not collect or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials
  • Your device identifiers, advertising ID, or unique IDs
  • Your location (precise or approximate)
  • Your contacts, photos, or any files on your device
  • Crash reports, performance data, or diagnostics
  • Quiz answers, scores, session history, or any usage data

All quiz content is bundled with the app at install time. Your answers, current streak, and session statistics are calculated on your device only and are not transmitted anywhere.

2. Permissions

Quiz App requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or any other protected device feature.

3. Third-Party Services

Quiz App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising networks, social sign-in providers, push notification services, or backend services. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network requests to the developer or any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

Quiz App is suitable for general audiences aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 because the app does not collect any information from anyone. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about the app, please contact us at the email below.

5. Data Security

Because Quiz App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data to secure beyond what stays on your device under your own control.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in app functionality or applicable law. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time from your device. Since no personal data is collected or stored externally, no further action is required to remove your information from our systems.
